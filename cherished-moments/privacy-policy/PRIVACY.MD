# Privacy Policy for Cherished Moments

**Effective Date:** January 2025

## Overview

Cherished Moments is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

**Key Privacy Principle: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

### Personal Information
- We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect device identifiers
- We do not collect usage analytics
- We do not collect crash reports

### Usage Data
- We do not track how you use the app
- We do not collect information about your events or moments
- We do not monitor your app behavior
- We do not collect search queries

### Device Information
- We do not access your device's unique identifiers
- We do not collect information about your device model, operating system, or other technical specifications
- We do not access your device's advertising ID

## How Your Data is Handled

### Local Storage Only
- All your events, moments, and data are stored exclusively on your device
- Data is saved using iOS's UserDefaults system
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties

### No Network Communication
- The app does not connect to the internet
- No data is uploaded or downloaded
- No remote servers are involved in data processing
- No cloud synchronization occurs

### No Third-Party Services
- We do not use analytics services (like Google Analytics)
- We do not use crash reporting services
- We do not use advertising networks
- We do not integrate with social media platforms

## Data Security

### Device-Level Protection
- Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- Data access is restricted to the app itself
- iOS sandbox security prevents unauthorized access

### No Data Transmission Risks
- Since no data leaves your device, there are no network security risks
- No risk of data breaches from external servers
- No risk of unauthorized access during transmission

## Your Control Over Data

### Complete Ownership
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete the app and all data will be removed
- You can manually delete individual events within the app

### Data Portability
- Since data is stored locally, it remains on your device
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data
- No data recovery is possible once the app is deleted

## Children's Privacy

- We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13
- Since we don't collect any data at all, this app is safe for children to use
- Parents can be confident that no personal information is being collected

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
- Any changes will be reflected in app updates
- The effective date will be updated accordingly
- Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes

## Legal Compliance

### GDPR Compliance
- Since we do not collect personal data, GDPR requirements do not apply
- No data processing activities occur that would require GDPR compliance

### CCPA Compliance
- We do not sell, share, or process personal information as defined by CCPA
- No personal information is collected that would be subject to CCPA

### Other Regulations
- Our no-data-collection approach ensures compliance with most privacy regulations worldwide

## Technical Implementation

### Data Storage
- Uses iOS UserDefaults for local data persistence
- No external databases or cloud storage
- Data format: Simple key-value pairs stored locally

### App Permissions
- The app does not request any special permissions
- No access to contacts, photos, location, or other sensitive data
- Minimal system permissions required for basic app functionality

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please note that:

- We cannot provide data deletion assistance since no data is collected
- We cannot provide data access since no data is stored externally
- For technical support, please refer to the app's support documentation

## Verification

You can verify our privacy claims by:
- Reviewing the app's source code (if available)
- Using network monitoring tools to confirm no data transmission
- Checking iOS privacy settings to see no permissions are requested

---

**Summary:** Cherished Moments is designed with privacy by design principles. We believe the best way to protect your privacy is to not collect your data in the first place. All your precious moments remain private and secure on your device.
